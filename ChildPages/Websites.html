Websites Webpage
Useful Websites:
  • 1. Planet minecraft has anything you want in it from mods to texturepacks.
  • 2. Namemc has a database which shows every account's name changes and names and past skins and it's commonly used to see if a name is available.
  • 3. Skindex is a place where people can make and share skins with others.
  • 4. Vanillatweaks is a custom resourcepack and datapack maker where you have about 50 options to choose from to make your own pack.
  • 5. Curseforge is where the majority of all minecraft mods and resource packs and datapacks are and it's also the most well known.
